Privacy Policy

How this informational website handles data. Last updated: June 2026.

This is a plain-language placeholder for OwnFi’s marketing website while the product is in development. It is not legal advice and may be updated as the product evolves.

Summary

This website is informational only. There are no user accounts, no login, no wallet connection and no application running here. We do not knowingly collect personal data beyond the basic technical logs described below.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect names, wallet addresses or financial information on this site.
  • We do not run analytics or advertising trackers.
  • We do not set tracking cookies.

Server logs

This site is hosted as static files (for example, on GitHub Pages). The hosting provider may automatically record standard technical information such as IP addresses, browser type and the pages requested, for security and operational purposes. This processing is governed by the hosting provider’s own privacy policy.

If you contact us or join the list

If you email us at hello@getownfi.com or submit your email address to the early-access list, we will use that information only to respond to you and to send product updates you asked for. If the early-access form is powered by a third-party service (such as a form provider), your submission is also handled under that provider’s privacy policy. You can ask us to delete your email at any time.

Third-party links

This site links to external sites such as GitHub. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes

We may update this policy as the OwnFi product develops. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “last updated” date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@getownfi.com.
